Write a Rust SpacetimeDB module that deletes a logical group in scheduled batches of at most two rows.

Define a public work_item table with struct WorkItem and fields id: u64 primary key and indexed group_id: u64. Define a private delete_job scheduled table with struct DeleteJob and fields scheduled_id: u64 auto-increment primary key, scheduled_at: ScheduleAt, and group_id: u64. Add seed_group(group_id, count), request_delete(group_id), and the scheduled reducer run_delete_batch. Each scheduled invocation may delete at most two matching items and must enqueue another one-shot job when items remain.
