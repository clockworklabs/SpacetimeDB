Write a C# SpacetimeDB module that deletes a logical group in scheduled batches of at most two rows.

Define a public WorkItem table with Id: ulong primary key and indexed GroupId: ulong. Define a private DeleteJob scheduled table with ScheduledId, ScheduledAt, and GroupId. Add SeedGroup(groupId, count), RequestDelete(groupId), and the scheduled reducer. Each scheduled invocation may delete at most two matching items and must enqueue another one-shot job when items remain.
