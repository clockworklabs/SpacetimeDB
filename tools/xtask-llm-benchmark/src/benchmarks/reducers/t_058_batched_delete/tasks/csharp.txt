Write a C# SpacetimeDB module that deletes a logical group in scheduled batches of at most two rows.

Define a public WorkItem table with Id: ulong primary key and indexed GroupId: ulong. Define a private DeleteJob scheduled table with ScheduledId: ulong auto-increment primary key, ScheduledAt: ScheduleAt, and GroupId: ulong. Add SeedGroup(groupId, count), RequestDelete(groupId), and the scheduled reducer RunDeleteBatch. Each scheduled invocation may delete at most two matching items and must enqueue another one-shot job when items remain.
