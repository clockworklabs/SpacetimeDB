Write a SpacetimeDB backend module in C# that deletes a logical group in scheduled batches of at most two rows.

TABLES
- WorkItem (public, accessor WorkItem)
  - Fields:
    - Id: ulong (primary key)
    - GroupId: ulong (btree index)
- DeleteJob (private, scheduled for RunDeleteBatch, accessor DeleteJob)
  - Fields:
    - ScheduledId: ulong (primary key, auto-increment)
    - ScheduledAt: ScheduleAt
    - GroupId: ulong

REDUCERS
- SeedGroup(groupId: ulong, count: uint)
  - Assign deterministic unique Id values to the inserted rows
- RequestDelete(groupId: ulong)
- RunDeleteBatch(job: DeleteJob) (scheduled reducer)
  - Delete at most two WorkItem rows matching the scheduled GroupId
  - If matching rows remain, enqueue another one-shot DeleteJob
