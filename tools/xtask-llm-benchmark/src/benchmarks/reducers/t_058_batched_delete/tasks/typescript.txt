Write a SpacetimeDB backend module in TypeScript that deletes a logical group in scheduled batches of at most two rows.

TABLES
- work_item (public)
  - Fields:
    - id: u64 (primary key)
    - groupId: u64 (btree index)
- delete_job (private, scheduled for runDeleteBatch)
  - Fields:
    - scheduledId: u64 (primary key, auto-increment)
    - scheduledAt: ScheduleAt
    - groupId: u64

REDUCERS
- seed_group(groupId: u64, count: u32)
- request_delete(groupId: u64)
- runDeleteBatch(timer: delete_job row) (scheduled reducer)
  - Delete at most two work_item rows matching the scheduled groupId
  - If matching rows remain, enqueue another one-shot delete_job
