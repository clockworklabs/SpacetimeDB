Write a TypeScript SpacetimeDB module that deletes a logical group in scheduled batches of at most two rows.

Define a public work_item table with id: u64 primary key and indexed groupId: u64. Define a private delete_job scheduled table with scheduledId: u64 auto-increment primary key, scheduledAt: ScheduleAt, and groupId: u64. Add seed_group(groupId, count), request_delete(groupId), and the scheduled reducer runDeleteBatch. Each scheduled invocation may delete at most two matching items and must enqueue another one-shot job when items remain.
