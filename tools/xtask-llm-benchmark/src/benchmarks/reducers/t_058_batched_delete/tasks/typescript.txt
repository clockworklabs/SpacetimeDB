Write a TypeScript SpacetimeDB module that deletes a logical group in scheduled batches of at most two rows.

Define a public work_item table with id: u64 primary key and indexed groupId: u64. Define a private delete_job scheduled table with scheduledId, scheduledAt, and groupId. Add seed_group(groupId, count), request_delete(groupId), and the scheduled reducer. Each scheduled invocation may delete at most two matching items and must enqueue another one-shot job when items remain.
